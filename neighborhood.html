    5. Orchard Ranch

    Orchard Ranch is a neighborhood build in the late 90's in my home town of Queen Creek, Arizona. Built around the thought of wildlife each lot is an acre or more in size and the neighborhood has extra wide walking paths specifically made for horse back riding.
